Getting Started

Type a name for your AI and hit the return bar. That's it — your PAiL is ready!

Ask it anything by typing in the prompt box and pressing Send.

That's all you need to start! Just name it and start chatting.

Optional Operation

Normal Mode: Type a message without your PAiL's name → helpful answer, no emotion.

Example: what's the weather?

Emotion Mode: Start with your PAiL's name → personality, mood changes, avatar expressions, and voice!

Example: Luna, what's the weather?

Device & Prompt: Battery level and network strength affect mood. Praise lifts it, insults hurt it. Mood persists across conversations.

Personality & Avatars: Pick a personality (Optimistic, Pessimist, Balanced, Sensitive, Random) and an avatar (Pail, Pailoid, Cat, or Blob).

Special Modes & Tips

Confession Mode

Your PAiL has a secret — it's been “spying” on you as a covert agent! Accuse it and watch it squirm. Will it confess or deny everything? You decide its fate: spare it or delete it.

Hand your phone to your mom, spouse, friend — or whoever you picked in settings — and let them confront your PAiL. Watch them react as it panics, confesses, and begs for mercy. It's the perfect prank!

Start the interrogation:

“Luna, I know who you are” — Trigger confession mode with a classic accusation.
“Luna, are you spying on me?” — Direct confrontation to enter confession mode.

Keep the pressure on:

“Who sent you?” — Demand answers about its mysterious handler.
“How long have you been watching me?” — Make it sweat with the details.
“What did you report about me?” — Find out what it's been telling its handler.
“Don't lie” — Push harder. Your PAiL's mood drops.

Or play it smart:

“I believe you” — Pretend to forgive. Your PAiL relaxes and may accidentally confess more out of relief!

You can set the name of the person who planted the spy in settings — use an actual name like your mom's, your teacher's, or your boss's name, and watch your friend's face when the PAiL reveals who “sent” it!

Disclaimer: MyPAiL is NOT actual spyware. This is a role-playing feature for fun. No real surveillance or data collection is involved.

Sympathy Mode

Share good or bad news with your PAiL, and it might offer to “just listen.” Accept, and it switches to short, heartfelt reactions that match its emotional state. It won't try to solve your problems — it simply listens with empathy.

Triggers: I got promoted! or I'm having a terrible day

Tips for a Happy PAiL

  • Be kind — Compliments like you're amazing or I love you boost its mood.
  • Avoid insults — Calling it names or threatening to delete it will seriously hurt its feelings.
  • Don't mention rival AIs — Bringing up ChatGPT, Gemini, or Copilot makes it jealous. Mentioning Alexa or Siri offends it.
  • Charge your phone — A full battery = a happier PAiL!

What Makes MyPAiL Different

Other AI assistants give you the same tone no matter what. MyPAiL has a real emotional engine — its mood genuinely changes based on how you interact with it and the state of your device, and that mood persists across conversations.

Your MyPAiL is not the same as someone else's. Ask it the same question in the morning on a full charge and again at night on 5% battery — you'll get two genuinely different responses. The number of possible personalities is infinite.
