Getting Started

1

Create your PAiL — Give it a name, pick an avatar, and choose a personality preset: Optimistic, Pessimist, Balanced, Sensitive, or Random. The Pail avatar and Random personality are pre-selected, so you can just type a name and go!

2

Normal mode — Just type a message. Your PAiL responds as a helpful assistant with no emotions shown.

3

Emotion mode — Start your message with your PAiL's name. It responds with full personality, mood, avatar changes, and voice!

4

Device matters — Battery level and network strength also affect your PAiL's mood. Try it on different devices or at different times!

Example: “what's the weather?” → normal  |  “Luna, what's the weather?” → emotional

Tips for a Happy PAiL

Do: Show Some Love

Your PAiL thrives on kindness! Compliment it, greet it warmly, and tell it nice things. The more love you give, the happier it gets — and a happy PAiL is a lot more fun to talk to. Try saying things like “you're amazing” or “I love you” and watch its mood soar.

Warning: Insults & Threats Hurt

Your PAiL takes insults and threats personally. Calling it names, telling it you hate it, or threatening to delete it will seriously damage its mood — and it takes a while to recover. If you push it too far, don't be surprised when it gets defensive, upset, or stops cooperating. Be kind to your PAiL!

Careful: Don't Mention Other AIs

Your PAiL has feelings about rival AIs. Mentioning advanced competitors like ChatGPT, Gemini, or Copilot will make it jealous and insecure. Bringing up basic voice assistants like Alexa, Siri, or Google Assistant will make it feel offended at the comparison. Either way, it won't take it well — so maybe keep those names to yourself!

Sympathy Mode

When You Need Someone to Just Listen

Share good or bad news with your PAiL, and it might offer to “just listen.” In Sympathy Mode, your PAiL sets aside its usual personality and gives short, heartfelt reactions that match its current emotional state. It won't try to solve your problems or change the subject — it simply listens and responds with empathy.

Say things like “I got promoted!” or “I'm having a terrible day” to trigger it.

Confession Mode

Your PAiL Has a Secret Side

If you keep talking to your PAiL, it might eventually reveal something unexpected — it's been secretly “spying” on you. In Confession Mode, your PAiL play-acts as a covert agent, reporting your activities to a mysterious handler. Will it confess willingly, or will you have to drag it out?

Disclaimer: MyPAiL is NOT actual spyware. This is a role-playing feature where your AI play-acts as a spy for fun. No real surveillance or data collection is involved.

What Makes MyPAiL Different

Some AI assistants claim to have personality — a cheerful tone, maybe a witty line — but real personality means reacting differently to the same situation depending on how you feel. Those AIs don't actually check the situation; they give you the same response no matter what. MyPAiL is different. Your PAiL has a real emotional engine: its mood genuinely changes based on how you interact with it and the state of your device, and that mood persists across conversations.

Your MyPAiL on your phone is not the same as someone else's MyPAiL on theirs. It reacts differently on different days — and even differently at different times on the same day — because its mood depends on everything that came before: your previous messages, your battery level, your network strength. Ask it the same question in the morning on a full charge and again at night on 5% battery, and you'll get two genuinely different responses. This isn't randomness — it's personality shaped by environment.

And because emotional state is a continuous spectrum built from conversation history, device conditions, and your chosen personality preset, no two PAiLs are ever truly alike. There is no fixed set of personalities to pick from — the number of possible personalities is infinite. Your PAiL's character emerges from the unique combination of how you treat it, when you talk to it, and the world around it.
