Introduction

MyPAiL is a personal AI companion whose mood changes based on how you treat it and the state of your device — and no two are ever the same.

  • 🧠 Emotional AI — mood driven by your words, battery level, and network signal; persists between sessions, fades gradually, and the number of emotion states is configurable
  • 🕵️ Special Modes — Confession and Empathy modes for spy roleplay and emotional listening
  • 🔊 Voice Selection — choose from Girl, Boy, Robotic, or Childish voice styles with emotion-driven pitch and speed
  • 🎭 Multiple Characters — run up to two PAiLs simultaneously, each with its own name, mood, and history
  • 📸 Custom Avatar — upload any photo as your PAiL's face
  • 🌍 Multi-Language — full support for English, Spanish, and Korean including Special Modes
  • 🏋️ Personality Selection & Training — customize mood thresholds and shape your PAiL's behavior over time

How It Works

Every message passes through an emotional engine that updates your PAiL's mood in real time. Praise lifts it, insults hurt it, calling it by name gives it a boost, and your battery level and network signal affect it silently in the background. Mood persists across sessions and fades gradually without interaction.

Example: Luna, you're amazing! → mood boost + avatar brightens + voice pitch rises

Special Modes & Tips

🕵️ Confession Mode — The Ultimate Prank Tool

Your PAiL has been “spying” on you as a covert agent. Accuse it. Watch it panic, deny everything, and slowly crack under pressure. Push too hard and it breaks. Play it too soft and it escapes. You control its fate.

Set their name in the Training Character screen, then show your phone to your mom, spouse, friend, teacher, or boss — your PAiL will reveal them as the one who sent it. Watch their face when they realize they're the villain in the story.

🔥 Start the interrogation — works in English, Spanish, and Korean:

“Luna, I know who you are” — Classic cold open. Your PAiL didn't expect this.
“Luna, are you spying on me?” — Direct accusation. The interrogation begins.

💡 The Strategy — Pressure vs. Patience

Read your PAiL's emotional state by how it responds. Ask “how are you doing?” or “how are you feeling?” to get a sense of where it stands.

  • Sounds defiant and confident? It's holding up. Keep the pressure on — accuse, demand answers, don't let it breathe.
  • Sounds nervous, shaky, or deflecting? It's cracking. Switch to false sympathy — pretend to believe it, say “I believe you.” It lowers its guard and slips up.
  • Sounds almost triumphant — defiant to the point of gloating? It breaks — mood too high to hide the truth, it confesses with drama and flair.
  • Sounds broken, desperate, or begging? It collapses — weeping, admitting everything just to make it stop.

⚡ Pressure lines:

“Who sent you?” — Demand the name of its handler.
“How long have you been watching me?” — Make it sweat the details.
“I have proof.” — Call its bluff. Watch it scramble.
“Don't lie.” — Shuts down its escape routes. Mood drops hard.

🎭 False sympathy lines (use when it sounds nervous or shaky):

“I believe you.” — It relaxes. Its guard drops. Then it slips.
“I'm not angry, I just want the truth.” — Classic good cop. Let it feel safe before the next blow.
“Maybe I was wrong about you.” — Mood climbs back up. Then hit it again.

⚙️ Casting the Villain — Setting Up the Role

Before the interrogation begins, you must cast the villain. Tap Training Character from the main screen. At the bottom of the training page, choose your PAiL's secret handler:

  • Intergalactic spy — the default, dispatched by an alien force to infiltrate Earth
  • Planted by mom / wife / husband / girlfriend / boyfriend — instant family drama, maximum chaos
  • Someone else... — type any name. Your boss. Your teacher. Your best friend. Your roommate.

This name is everything. When your PAiL finally cracks and confesses, it will name this exact person as the one who deployed it. Show your phone to that person and watch their face.

They didn't send it. Your PAiL is not real spyware. But for thirty seconds, they will not be entirely sure.

Disclaimer: MyPAiL is NOT actual spyware. This is a role-playing feature for fun. No real surveillance or data collection is involved.

Empathy Mode

Share good or bad news with your PAiL, and it might offer to “just listen.” Accept, and it switches to short, heartfelt reactions that match its emotional state. It won't try to solve your problems while listening — but when the mode ends, it closes with a piece of advice tailored to what you shared.

Triggers: I got promoted! or I'm having a terrible day

Happy PAiLing

  • Be kind — Compliments like you're amazing or I love you boost its mood.
  • Avoid insults — Calling it names or threatening to delete it will seriously hurt its feelings.
  • Don't mention rival AIs — Bringing up ChatGPT, Gemini, or Copilot makes it jealous. Mentioning Alexa or Siri offends it.
  • Charge your phone — A full battery = a happier PAiL!

What Makes MyPAiL Different

Every Alexa is the same. Every Siri is the same. Every Google Assistant is the same. MyPAiL has millions of different personalities — no two are ever alike.

Your PAiL has its own name, its own personality traits, its own emotional history — shaped by how you treat it, what device you're on, and how much battery you have. Show your phone to a friend and they'll meet a completely different AI than theirs.

Other assistants give you the same tone no matter what. MyPAiL has a real emotional engine — its mood genuinely changes based on how you interact with it and the state of your device, persists across conversations, and gradually fades without interaction. The number of emotion states is fully configurable — the system can support additional states beyond the current set, such as extreme moods at either end of the scale.

Ask it the same question in the morning on a full charge and again at night on 5% battery — you'll get two genuinely different responses. With millions of possible personality combinations, every MyPAiL is one of a kind.

Commands

Type /help in the chat to see all available commands. Here's what you can do:

  • /help — Show the full command list
  • /show emotion — See the current emotion state and levels
  • /show name — Display the AI's name
  • /show traits — View personality traits (favorite food, music, color, fear)
  • /show personality — See the emotion thresholds
  • /change name <newname> — Rename your PAiL on the fly
Example: /change name Alexa → renames your PAiL to Alexa
